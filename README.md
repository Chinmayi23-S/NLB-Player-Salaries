# NLB-Player-Salaries
Statistical Analysis of Salaries of National league baseball players.
Interpreting measure of central tendency and using multiple regression analysis to check dependency of 2 independent variables
     -Playing Experience with Current Team (years)
     -Total Years of Experience

Skills Showed: #ExcelDataAnalysisTool 
               #StatisticalAnalysis 
               #InterpretatingModel 
               #RegressionAnalysis

Key Insights
R-squared value = 0.0055 Therefore, 0.5% of the variance in the salary is explained by the model. 
Significance: F = 0.088 (p value) which is more than 0.05. So, the model is statistically not significant. 

p-value of ‘Playing Experience with Current Team (years)’ = 0.0287
p-value of ‘Total Years of Experience’ = 0.0275

The p-values of both independent variables are significantly less than 0.05. Indicates that the corresponding regression coefficient is statistically significant, implying that the independent variables have a meaningful impact on the players salaries.
The regression model is statistically significant.
